Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NT Survey Platform collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website. By creating an account, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We collect your name and email address during the registration process to identify your account.
  • Technical Data: We automatically collect your IP address, browser type, and device information to monitor for security threats.
  • Survey Progress: We track your completed tasks and coin balance to ensure accurate rewards.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for operational purposes. This includes verifying your identity for withdrawals, preventing fraudulent activities, and providing customer support via WhatsApp or Email. We never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers.

1.1 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to maintain your login session and to track survey completion via postback URLs. Third-party offerwalls may also use cookies to prevent duplicate task completions.

1.2 Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13.

3. Data Security and Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication and Database systems. While we implement strict security measures, please be aware that no transmission over the internet is completely immune to risks.

4. Strict Anti-Fraud Policy

To protect our survey partners, we have a zero-tolerance policy for fraud. The use of VPNs, Proxies, or creating multiple accounts from the same device is strictly prohibited. Any detected violation will result in an immediate and permanent account ban without prior notice.

5. Third-Party Links and Offerwalls

Our platform contains links to third-party offerwalls and survey providers. Once you click on these links, you are subject to their specific privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data collection practices of these external partners.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us through our official support channels on the Contact page.
